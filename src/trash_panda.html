Trash Panda

Due to the increase of plastic waste during the pandemic, people who are conscious about their waste looked for another way to dispose their recyclables besides dropping it off to facilities or drop-off points. Trash panda offers a pick-up service for their recyclables.

To further understand their unique personality, needs and pain points, I reached out to people who left a comment on Trash panda’s Facebook posts and invited them for interview or usability testing. This led me for a partial redesign to easily find information and have a systematic way to cancel booking.

Why the redesign?

Although users find the app pretty straight-forward and easy to use because of it’s simplicity, they still tend to look for information through their Facebook page, website, or their email when they can’t access those information through the app.

Prior doing user research, I discovered that there is no means for the user to cancel their booking in the app. Users will, instead, cancel their booking by messaging their Facebook page. For some instances, this can also have an impact to Trash panda’s resources since fees are not paid upfront upon booking.

The process

My first step in research is by reading through comments on their Facebook page to see the bigger picture first and used this opportunity to reach out to invite them for interview or usability testing.
